About me

Project Description

An exciting project was proposed focusing on the study of silhouettes to create an illustration of a Japanese Yokai, specifically the Abura-sumashi.
This demon from Japanese folklore is known for its mysterious and eerie appearance, and I dedicated time and effort to capture its essence through suggestive shapes and distinctive contours. This project allowed me to explore the rich Japanese mythology and refine my artistic skills in the field of digital illustration, offering a new perspective on a figure as fascinating as it is enigmatic.
